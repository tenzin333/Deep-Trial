DeepTrail

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026  ·  Extension v1.0.2
DeepTrail is a Chrome Extension that builds a personal AI knowledge graph from your browsing history. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, how we store it, and what controls you have. By installing DeepTrail, you agree to the practices described here.
1

Data We Collect

1.1 Account Data

DataWhy We Collect It
Email addressRequired to create and identify your account
PasswordFor authentication — stored only as a bcrypt hash, never in plain text

1.2 Browsing Activity Data

When you save a page (manually with Ctrl+Shift+S or automatically with auto-save enabled):

DataWhy We Collect It
Page URLTo identify and de-duplicate pages in your knowledge graph
Page titleTo display a readable label for each node in your graph
Extracted page text (up to 30 KB)To generate AI summaries, keywords, and semantic embeddings
Timestamp of visitTo order and display your captured pages

1.3 Locally Stored Data (On Your Device Only)

DataStorage LocationPurpose
JWT authentication tokenchrome.storage.localKeeps you signed in across sessions
Email addresschrome.storage.localDisplays your email in the extension header
Auto-save preferencechrome.storage.localRemembers your auto-save on/off setting
API configurationchrome.storage.localStores the backend server URL
2

Data We Do NOT Collect

DeepTrail intentionally avoids capturing the following:
  • Content from Google services (Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Accounts, Google Search) — explicitly excluded by the extension
  • Content from Chrome internal pages (chrome://, about:blank, etc.) — explicitly excluded
  • Content from the Chrome Web Store — explicitly excluded
  • Passwords in plain text (only bcrypt hashes are stored server-side)
  • Financial information, credit card or banking data
  • Health or medical information
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, calendar entries, or photos
  • Audio, video, or microphone input
  • Any data from pages you have not chosen to save
3

How We Use Your Data

DataHow It Is Used
Email + password hashAccount authentication and login
Saved URLs, titles, timestampsBuilding your knowledge graph; displaying Activity Feed
Extracted page textInput for AI summarization and semantic embedding generation
AI summaries and keywordsDisplayed in your graph and Activity Feed
Vector embeddingsComputing similarity scores to automatically link related pages
Your search queriesFinding semantically relevant pages and generating AI answers
We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to train AI models. Data is passed to third-party APIs transiently for processing only.
4

Third-Party Services

DeepTrail uses the following third-party services to provide AI features. We do not sell your data to any of these parties.

ServiceData SentPurposePrivacy Policy
Google Gemini API Extracted page text (up to 30 KB per page) Generates 768-dimensional semantic embeddings for similarity matching Google Privacy Policy
Groq API (LLaMA-3.3-70b) (1) First 4 KB of page text for summarization. (2) Your search question + relevant summaries for AI answers. Generates page summaries, keywords, and answers your natural-language queries Groq Privacy Policy
Jina Reader API Page URL only (no page content) Extracts readable text from JavaScript-heavy pages when standard parsing fails Jina AI Privacy Policy
Note: Content sent to these services is transmitted transiently for processing only. DeepTrail does not instruct these providers to use your data to train their models.

4.1 Data Sharing — Summary

DeepTrail shares user data only with the third-party processors listed above, and only to the extent needed to deliver the features you trigger. Specifically, DeepTrail does not:

  • Sell or rent your personal or browsing data to anyone;
  • Transfer your data to data brokers, advertising networks, or for any advertising/marketing purpose;
  • Share your data with third parties for purposes unrelated to the extension's single core function;
  • Use or transfer your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

4.2 Limited Use Compliance

DeepTrail's collection, use, and transfer of user data complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. All user data is used solely to provide and improve the extension's single purpose — building and querying your personal research knowledge graph — and for security and legal-compliance purposes where required.
5

Data Storage & Security

AspectDetail
Storage locationPostgreSQL database hosted on Render (United States)
Password securityAll passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a unique salt before storage. Plain-text passwords are never stored or logged.
Data in transitAll communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
AuthenticationJWT tokens with short expiry windows; tokens are stored only in your browser's local storage, not on our servers
Embeddings768-dimensional vectors derived from page content are stored for similarity search; they cannot be reverse-engineered to recover original text
Analytics & trackingWe do not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, or crash-reporting tools that access user data independently
6

User Controls & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

ActionHow To Do It
Disable auto-saveSettings tab → toggle "Auto-Save Pages" off. Only pages you manually trigger (Ctrl+Shift+S) will be saved.
Delete a single pageGraph tab → click a node → select Delete
Delete all saved dataGraph tab → "Clear All" button — permanently removes all your nodes, summaries, embeddings, and connections
Sign outSettings tab → "Sign Out" — removes your authentication token from the browser
Delete your accountEmail tenthinlay007@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" — all account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
Note: Deleting all nodes removes all associated summaries, keywords, embeddings, and graph connections. This action cannot be undone.

DeepTrail is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us at tenthinlay007@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.
